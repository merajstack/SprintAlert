   $ cd /Users/mohammedmehraj/app

3. Verify files:
   $ ./verify.sh

   $ ./gradlew assembleRelease

5. Find your APK:
   /Users/mohammedmehraj/app/app/build/outputs/apk/release/app-release.apk

6. Install on device:
   $ adb install app/build/outputs/apk/release/app-release.apk
